Meet the team behind Versa Spotting Group

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Versa Spotting Group began as a small group of aviation enthusiasts who shared a passion for aircraft photography. What started as a hobby quickly grew into a dedicated community of spotters, photographers, and aviation professionals.

Today, we are one of the most recognized aviation spotting groups in the world, with members spanning across multiple continents. Our mission is to capture and share the beauty of aviation while promoting safety and respect for the industry.
